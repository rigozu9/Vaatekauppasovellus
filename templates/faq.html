Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Question 1: How are the items authenticated?

At CDCA we have an expert authentication team that checks every single listing to provide quaranteed satisfaction for buyers.

Question 2: What are the buyer/seller fees in CDCA?

For buyers there arent no fees. And sellers pay 5% for the garment they sold.

Question 3: What are the shipping fees in CDCA?

There are no shipping fees. After you sell an item we will provide a shipping code for you.
